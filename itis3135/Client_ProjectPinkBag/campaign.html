Successful Campaign

Our first successful PinkBag campaign held in June 2022. During this campaign, we provided 60 girls with a full a year of sanitary at KUIH High School in Gopalgonj. We also gave some educational materials to the other boys and girls so they don't feel left out. In the future, we aim to conduct workshops and awareness programs to promote menstrual health education and empower women with the knowledge they need. The campaign not only provided essential supplies but also initiated important conversations and broke down barriers, furthering our mission to create lasting change.
