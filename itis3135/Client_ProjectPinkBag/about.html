About US

Project PinkBag's impact goes beyond the distribution of menstrual supplies. We are actively engaged in community outreach and education initiatives to foster a culture of openness and understanding around menstruation. Our team conducts workshops and awareness programs in schools, community centers, and rural areas to dispel myths, challenge taboos, and promote menstrual health literacy. By addressing the root causes of misinformation and stigma, we aim to empower girls and women with knowledge, ensuring they can make informed decisions about their health.

In addition to our educational efforts, Project PinkBag is dedicated to promoting sustainable practices. We are exploring eco-friendly alternatives to traditional menstrual products to reduce our environmental footprint. By advocating for and providing access to reusable and environmentally conscious options, we not only contribute to the well-being of individuals but also advocate for a healthier planet. Through these holistic approaches, Project PinkBag strives to create lasting change and build a future where menstruation is embraced as a natural and essential aspect of women's health.

We believe in a future where every girl and woman can manage her menstrual health with confidence and pride. With the support of our donors, volunteers, and partners, we are making strides toward achieving this vision. Together, we can create a world where menstrual hygiene is not just a necessity but a catalyst for empowerment and gender equality.

As we continue our journey, we invite individuals, businesses, and organizations to join us in this meaningful cause. Together, we can break down the barriers that hinder menstrual health and women's empowerment, fostering a world where every girl and woman can thrive with dignity, confidence, and equality.
