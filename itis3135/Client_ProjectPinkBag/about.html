About US

Project PinkBag is a non-profit organization dedicated to menstrual hygiene and women's empowerment in Bangladesh. Our team consists of passionate individuals who are committed to making a positive impact. We work tirelessly to break down the barriers that girls and women face due to limited access to menstrual supplies and the stigma surrounding menstruation. Through our distribution of sanitary products and educational programs, we strive to improve the overall well-being and self-esteem of the girls and women we serve.

We believe in a future where every girl and woman can manage her menstrual health with confidence and pride. With the support of our donors, volunteers, and partners, we are making strides toward achieving this vision. Together, we can create a world where menstrual hygiene is not just a necessity but a catalyst for empowerment and gender equality.
