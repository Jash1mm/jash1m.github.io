Donate to Support Menstrual Hygiene and Women's Empowerment Your generous donation can make a real difference in the lives of girls and women in Bangladesh. By contributing to Project PinkBag, you are helping us ensure that every individual has access to safe and hygienic menstrual supplies, empowering them to manage their periods with dignity. Your support can break down barriers, foster education, and create a brighter future for countless girls and women.

Ways to Donate:

One-Time Donation: Your one-time donation will provide immediate assistance in delivering menstrual hygiene kits and educational resources to those in need. Every contribution, no matter how small, counts. Monthly Giving: Consider becoming a monthly donor to provide ongoing support for our programs. Your recurring donations sustain our efforts and enable us to plan for the long term, ensuring lasting impact. Sponsor a Girl: You can make a significant impact by sponsoring a girl's menstrual hygiene kit for an entire year. This sponsorship guarantees consistent access to supplies and education, helping a young girl thrive. Corporate Sponsorship: If you represent a business or organization, consider partnering with us to support our cause. Your support can make a substantial difference and demonstrate your commitment to social responsibility.

How Your Donation Helps:

$10: Provides a month's supply of sanitary pads for one girl. $25: Offers menstrual hygiene education and supplies for one woman. $50: Sponsors a girl's menstrual hygiene kit for an entire year. $100: Enables us to conduct awareness programs and workshops for a group of girls and women. Custom Amount: Your custom donation amount can be directed to where it's needed most. Your donation is tax-deductible, and we are committed to ensuring that every dollar you contribute goes directly to our programs. We believe in transparency and accountability, and we're grateful for your trust in our mission.

Together, we can make a lasting impact on menstrual hygiene and women's empowerment. Thank you for your support!
