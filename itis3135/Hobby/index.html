Welcome to JH Production

My film making journey started when I was in 12th grade back in my home country, Bangladesh. I met Anup and Ismail at the school, later found out Anup is into film making, and he intrigued us into creating films with him.

We created a few projects together and it was really fun. We borrowed camera, micropohone, and built light panels ourselves. We kept going however we could becasue it felt natural and fun to do. It's 2023 and Anup lives in China, Ismail is in Bangladesh, and I am in the United Staes of America. Even though all of us are in different places, we still have the urge to make videos individually. I guess film making / making videos became the second nature in our lives now.
