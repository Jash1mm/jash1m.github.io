Website Evaluations

The CRAP principal helps to design a great website and helps to distinguish from others. What is CRAP?

C (Contrast) R (Repetition) A (Alignment) P (Proximity)

Great Website --> apple.com

  • C - They used white background and black foreground, which makes it easy to read and draws attention. Besides they also use appropriate size in many places for better visibility and readability.

  • R - Colors, fonts, sizes of fonts, shapes, and line thickness on this website is perfectly matched in througout the entrie website.

  • A - They have a "Center" alignment in all the pages which visually draws attention and keeps the viewer engaged.

  • P - Proper proximity is used in their website, it does not feel like crowded, and it makes it easy to read.

Bad Website --> lingcars.com

  • C - The background and foreground is extremly busy and use of multiple different colors creates an unpleasant contrast.

  • R - Their repetions are scattered all around the place and makes it difficult to read.

  • A - Alignments are sometime centered and sometime left sided which makes it difficult for the audience to read.

  • P - Proximity in this website is extremly tight to each other and gives a headache.
