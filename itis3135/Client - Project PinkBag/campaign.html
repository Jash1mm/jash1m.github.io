Successful Campaign

One of our most successful campaigns to date was our "PinkCare 2022" initiative. During this campaign, we distributed over 100,000 menstrual hygiene kits to girls and women in remote and underserved areas of Bangladesh. These kits included sanitary pads, menstrual cups, and educational materials. In addition, we conducted workshops and awareness programs to promote menstrual health education and empower women with the knowledge they need. The campaign not only provided essential supplies but also initiated important conversations and broke down barriers, furthering our mission to create lasting change.
